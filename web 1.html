Virtual reality (VR) is an immersive technology that places users inside digital worlds using a headset (HMD) and motion-tracked controllers. VR creates 360-degree visuals and spatial audio, reacting to head and hand movements, allowing users to interact with virtual environments as if they were real.
Key Elements:
-HMD & Tracking Sensors: Provides immersive visuals and tracks body movements in 3D space.
-Haptic Feedback & Spatial Audio:Adds touch sensations and directional sound for realism.
